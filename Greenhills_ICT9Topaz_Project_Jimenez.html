March is one of the warmest months in the Philippines. It is also the month where a lot of fire incidents happen. This is why its considered fire prevention month here in the Philippines, so here are some ways to prevent fires.


Ways to Prevent Fire
Its important to put and maintain smoke alarms across every floor and in bedrooms. Many fire deaths and incidents happen when people are asleep.
Never leave cooking equipment like stoves, fryers, deep fryers, and ovens as these equipment causing fires if unnatended or left open for too long.
Avoid using fixed and portable heaters because heating equipment is one of the leading causes of house fires.
Avoid smoking inside houses as its smoke can activate smoke detectors and alarms as well as be a fire hazard.
